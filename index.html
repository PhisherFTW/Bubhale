Focus Areas

  • Pistol rounds (and ecos) try to have more calm aim and focus on tapping for the head instead of spamming.
  • When attempting to trade a teammate try to stay within tradeable distance at all times because if you are too far away your teammate could die without you having a chance to trade.
  • Only pull-out utility when you are certain you cannot get peeked or have cleared all positions that pose a threat to you, and try not to throw LOS(Line of sight) utility.
  • Do not stick the plant if you think an enemy is going to peek you (only do this if there is no-time to plant in a different place such as less than 7 seconds left on the clock.)
  • When peeking, peek with full focus and like you expect and enemy to be at every angle you peek.
  • When throwing utility make sure you take your time to get the lineup right because if you don’t wasted utility is worse than unused utility.
